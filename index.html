Consumer Protection
Buy Genuine — Beware of Fakes

⚠️ Red Flags — Avoid These

  • 🚨No BIS IS mark on the bar
  • 🚨No Mill Test Certificate (MTC)
  • 🚨Price 10%+ below market rate
  • 🚨Dealer not on sailsuraksha.com
  • 🚨No SAIL SeQR embossing on bar
  • 🚨Advance payment to unknown party
  • 🚨Phone-based factory discount claims
  • 🚨No weight slip or quality report

✅ How to Verify Genuine

  • ✅Check ISI mark & BIS cert on bar
  • ✅Demand MTC with SAIL stamp
  • ✅Verify dealer at sailsuraksha.com
  • ✅SAIL SEQR embossed every 1 metre
  • ✅Invoice with dealer GSTIN
  • ✅Cross-check weight with grade table
  • ✅NABL test for large purchases
  • ✅Call 1800-11-7475 to verify dealer
